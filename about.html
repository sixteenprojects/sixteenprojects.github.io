The Sixteen Project

Threat Hunting & Intelligence Platform

What is The Sixteen Project?

The Sixteen Project is an internal threat intelligence and threat hunting platform built to centralize and share knowledge about threats, techniques, and tools relevant to our security operations.

What's inside?

  • Threat Library — 2,300+ malware families, APT groups, and offensive tools, enriched with IOC data and MITRE ATT&CK mappings.
  • Threat Reports — in-depth investigation and analysis reports produced by the team.
  • Threat Hunting Tools — tools and scripts developed to support threat hunting and incident response.

Data Sources

Threat library data is sourced from open-source intelligence (OSINT) repositories:

  • ETDA APT Groups & Tools
  • Malpedia (Fraunhofer FKIE)
  • ThreatFox (abuse.ch)
  • MITRE ATT&CK
